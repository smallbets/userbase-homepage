Who's this?

Userbase is a small independent business, based in Seattle, USA. We offer a simple web service to make it very easy to create secure web apps.

And that's it. We're not in it to dominate the market, conquer the competition, or disrupt anything. This is a simple product, and we intend to keep it simple. We have no illusions of grandeur, and we're structured to be lean, profitable, and sustainable. We're not beholden to any investors, and we're here for the long haul.

If you have any questions, or if we can do anything to help you with your web app, please get in touch.
